Open and affirming to all genders, sexualities, bodies, relationship styles, and ways of existing as a human being in the world.

Code of Conduct:

Respect for femininity

This site makes no effort to draw a line around the category of “woman”, but anyone who feels that a part of themselves is feminine deserves to have that part be respected.

Respect for people of color

Non-white folks deserve online spaces where they are treated like their complete selves, not just members of their race. This should be such a space. To the extent your sexuality involves a racial component, express the desires not the aversions.

Consent is an ongoing process

Boundaries can exist at any point on any level. Be prepared to acknowledge and respect them.

Respect for genders and sexualities outside the ones you currently understand

It’s a special kind of insult to tell someone that something they've struggled to understand about themselves is invalid.

Accept people’s bodies as they are

No one’s body is wrong, only the mistreatment of them because of their body. This includes secondary sexual characteristics, primary sexual characteristics, weight, age, ableness, &c.

No threats of violence

Unless you're engaging in a mutually-sought-after BDSM style thing, violent speech has no place here. If someone doesn't understand that you are joking, you’re in the wrong, not them.

Be human

No robots, monsters, catfish, energy vampires, or trolls.
